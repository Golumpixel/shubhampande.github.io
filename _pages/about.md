I am an Assistant Professor in the [Department of Electronics Engineering](https://www.iitbhu.ac.in/dept/ece) at [IIT (BHU) Varanasi](https://www.iitbhu.ac.in/). Previously, I was a Device Modeling Engineer at [Intel Technology India](https://www.intel.in/), Hyderabad, where I worked on PDK development for nanosheet CMOS devices with backside power delivery for the 14A/10A process nodes.

I received my Direct PhD (MS + PhD) from [IIT Madras](https://www.iitm.ac.in/) in 2025, advised by [Prof. Anjan Chakravorty](https://www.ee.iitm.ac.in/anjan/) and [Prof. Bhaswar Chakrabarti](https://www.ee.iitm.ac.in/bhaswar/). My thesis focused on ReRAM Memory Technology for Neuromorphic Computing — spanning device design, compact modelling, and circuit-level applications. I was a [Fulbright-Nehru Doctoral Research Fellow](https://www.usief.org.in/Fellowships/Fulbright-Nehru-Doctoral-Research-Fellowships.aspx) at [UC Santa Barbara](https://www.ucsb.edu/) (2022–23), where I worked with [Prof. Dmitri Strukov](https://strukov.ece.ucsb.edu/) on temperature-modulated synaptic devices for spiking neural networks.

## Research Interests

- Advanced CMOS Devices (nanosheet, FinFET, GAAFET)
- Novel Memory Concepts (ReRAM, FeFET)
- Neuromorphic Hardware and Compute-in-Memory
- Design–Technology Co-Optimization (DTCO)
